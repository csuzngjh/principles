**Key assertions**:
- After second markTaskRetryWait: task.status='max_attempts_exceeded'
- task.attemptCount=2 (no longer incrementable via lease)
- Future acquireLease throws or returns task with status 'max_attempts_exceeded'

To trigger shouldRetry=false: call markTaskRetryWait on a task where attemptCount >= maxAttempts. Verify the transition to max_attempts_exceeded.
