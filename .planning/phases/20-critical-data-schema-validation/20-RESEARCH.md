# Phase 20 Research

## Mode

Brownfield production hardening.

No exploratory research spike is required before planning because:

- the affected data contracts are already known
- the immediate need is to centralize and enforce, not to discover new capability
- current production failures already identify the weak boundaries

## Working Assumption

Prefer small internal validators and explicit typed parsers first.

Do not introduce a large schema framework unless it materially reduces risk in the touched paths.
