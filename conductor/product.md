# Principles Disciple: Product Guide

## 1. Product Vision
To transform AI assistants from simple task-executors into self-evolving digital lifeforms capable of autonomous self-defense, deep reflection, and continuous cognitive improvement.

## 2. Target Users
- **AI Engineers & Developers**: Who need robust guardrails and self-correcting agents for complex software engineering tasks.
- **AI Safety Researchers**: Exploring radical concepts in AI cognition and alignment within a sandbox environment.
- **Power Users**: Who want to automate large-scale projects with minimal supervision and high reliability.

## 3. Core Features
- **Progressive Gatekeeper (Safety)**: A dynamic, trust-based security system that automatically adjusts modification permissions (4-stages) based on historical performance and modification risk.
- **Thinking OS (Meta-Cognition)**: A layered cognitive framework (Thinking OS, Principles, Procedures) that ensures architectural consistency.
- **Pain Signals & Evolution**: A biological-inspired feedback loop that captures failures ("pain") and triggers autonomous reflection or evolution.
- **Strategic Alignment (OKR)**: Synchronizes agent actions with high-level user objectives through a formal OKR system.

## 4. Key Goals
- **Minimize Human Oversight**: Shift the human role from code reviewer to cognitive architect.
- **Prevent Technical Debt**: Enforce architectural beauty and long-term maintainability through mandatory planning and auditing.
- **Autonomous Recovery**: Enable agents to identify their own deadlocks and dead-ends, and proactively call for specialized "Evolver" assistance.
